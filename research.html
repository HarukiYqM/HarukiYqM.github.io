Research

I am interested in various AI topics: Adversarial Machine learning, Computer Vision and Deep learning Theory. I think the current development of computer vision fell into a wrong way. Without supports from theory, new models come out with permuatations and combinations of different "building blocks" which results in a few crucial problems:
  • Why does the model work? (No body know yet)
  • Does there exists the best model, i.e, best permutation of exsiting units? In fact, people can design a better model.
    • Without answering above two question, we will never full utilize the true power of deep learning.
